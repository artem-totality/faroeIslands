Unspoiled, Unexplored & Unbelievable
Discover

The Faroe Islands are located in the Northeast Atlantic half way
between Scotland and Iceland - only two hours flight from mainland Europe

Adrift in the whirling rhythmic North Atlantic Sea,
a different world lingers. A place like no other on earth.
An idyllic escape, peacefully set among lush green valleys,
imposing basalt cliffs and waterfalls plunging directly
into the wind whipped ocean

The 18 jagged volcanic islands resemble a handful of rocks
scattered haphazardly in the deep-sea ocean.
Home to mountains of myth, hobbit-like turf-roofed houses
and grazing shaggy sheep, these islands make up
the perfect playground for the senses
